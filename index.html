FAQ

Common questions.

Does this work on iPad and Android tablets?
Yes. On iOS, FoodTruckPOS runs on any iPad with iOS 16 or later — including iPad mini, iPad Air, iPad Pro, and the standard iPad. On Android, it runs on any Android tablet with Android 8 or later. No specific model required on either platform.
Do I need internet to take orders?
No. The app works fully offline. Cash payments process instantly. Card payments queue and sync automatically when connectivity returns.
How does the kitchen display work?
The cashier and kitchen iPads connect over your local WiFi or iPhone hotspot using peer to peer networking. No internet required between the two devices.
What payment methods are supported?
Cash, or confirm card payments taken on your own Square Reader or any external card terminal with manual confirmation. Most operators use cash plus one card option.
Can I use my existing Square reader?
Yes. Select Square Reader as your payment method. The app records the payment and prompts your staff to charge on the Square hardware separately.
Is my data safe?
All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. Payment processing goes through Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified. We never store card numbers.
Is there an Android version?
Yes — launching on Google Play July 26 for Android tablets running Android 8 or later. The same account works on both iPad and Android.
Can I use both iPad and Android at the same time?
Yes. Pro and Enterprise plans support multiple devices. Your owner can use an iPad while your cashier uses an Android tablet, all on the same account.
